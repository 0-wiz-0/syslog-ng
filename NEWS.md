3.33.2
======

## Bugfixes

 * `disk-buffer`: fixed a bug, which was introduced in 3.33.1, where we
   sometimes corrupted the disk-buffer file when it reached full size.
   ([#3726](https://github.com/syslog-ng/syslog-ng/pull/3726))
